Arnold Schwarzenegger

Arnold Schwarzenegger, standing with his arms open widely.

Major events in the life of the greatest bodybuilder ever:

  • 1947 - Born near Graz, Austria.
  • 1961 - Started to go gym for the first time.
  • 1968 - Moved to United States.
  • 1976 - Received a Golden Globe Award for Best Newcomer for his performance in "Stay Hungry".
  • 1977 - Starred in documentary "Pumping Iron".
  • 1984 - Starred as a deadly machine from the future in "The Terminator".
  • 1991 - Starred in "Terminator 2: Judgment Day".
  • 2003 - Starred in "Terminator 3: Rise of the Machines".
  • 2003 - Elected as 38th Governor of California.
  • 2006 - Re-elected as governor.
  • 2010 - Starred in the film "The Expendables".
  • 2012 - Starred for a follow-up film, "The Expendables 2".
  • 2014 - Rejoined Sylvester Stallone for "The Expendables 3".
  • 2015 - Starred in "Terminator Genisys" with Jason Clarke and Emilia Clarke.
